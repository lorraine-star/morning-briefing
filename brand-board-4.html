The Vibe

High-contrast, high-impact. This direction leads with authority — the near-black backgrounds make everything feel decisive and premium. The warm coral accent keeps it from feeling cold or corporate, adding a human pulse to the bold foundation. Think: someone who's quiet but when they walk in, the room shifts. The Playfair Display headlines command attention; the Urbanist body text keeps everything modern and readable.

vs. Current Brand: The biggest departure of all five boards. No blue at all — this replaces the tech-startup energy with something closer to a luxury personal brand. The dark backgrounds are extremely striking on social media (they stop the scroll). The tradeoff: dark-mode aesthetics can feel less approachable if not balanced carefully, and this requires the most asset rework. But for premium course pricing and enterprise clients, it signals "top tier" immediately.
