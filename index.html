Confident Communication Tip — #12 of 20

CC Tip #12
Claim the Pause
Before answering a tough question on stage or in a meeting, take one intentional breath. That 2-second pause signals confidence, buys you real thinking time, and shifts the energy in the room. Most people rush to fill silence — leaders own it.

How to use it today: In your LinkedIn Learning call at 10am, when Melissa asks about your course vision, count to two before you answer. Notice how the room listens more closely. The pause isn't weakness — it's authority.
