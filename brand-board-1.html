The Vibe

This direction keeps your brand recognizable while dialing up the warmth and sophistication. The blues are slightly deeper and richer — less tech-startup, more trusted authority. Swapping the bright yellow for a warm gold adds gravitas without losing energy. It says: "I've been doing this, and I'm only getting better."

vs. Current Brand: Same family of blues but warmer and more intentional. Gold replaces yellow for a more premium feel. The serif headline font adds editorial weight that the current brand doesn't have. Overall, this is the lowest-risk evolution — immediately recognizable as Lorraine, just elevated.
