The Vibe

This is the introvert's brand — and it's intentional. The muted sage and blush palette whispers rather than shouts, creating a feeling of calm authority. Every element breathes. The generous white space, the lighter font weights, the soft gradients — it all says "I'm confident enough that I don't need to be loud." Libre Baskerville in italic feels like a handwritten letter from someone wise; Jost keeps the body text modern and effortlessly readable.

vs. Current Brand: The furthest from the current brand in energy. Where the current blues feel active and tech-forward, this palette feels grounded and introspective. It leans fully into the introvert identity as a strength. The potential risk: muted palettes can get lost on social media where everything is fighting for attention. But for someone with 332K followers and growing course revenue, the audience is already listening — the brand just needs to match the message. This is the direction that would feel most authentically "Lorraine" if the introvert brand is the long-term play.
